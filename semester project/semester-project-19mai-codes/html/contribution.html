Be a Hero! Help Your Community Science Museum

Your support makes the museum a magical place for discovery and learning. Here are fun ways you can help!

Every donation lets us build new exhibits and make science fun for everyone. Even a small gift can do big things!

Want to be more involved? Join our team of awesome volunteers. Help out with our exhibits, lead tours, or support our workshops.

Companies and individuals can also sponsor our exhibits or educational programs. It’s a great way to show you care about science and education while getting your name out there.

We’re super grateful for all the support from our community. Thanks to you, we now host free admission days every month, welcome thousands of extra visitors, and have expanded our Science After School program to reach more kids!
