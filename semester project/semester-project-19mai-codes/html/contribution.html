Support the Community Science Museum

Your support helps us bring exciting science to life for children and families! There are many ways you can contribute to our community.

Your donations help us create new exhibits, offer educational programs, and maintain our facilities. Every little bit helps!

You can Join our volunteer team to make a difference in our community. Volunteers help with exhibit maintenance, guide visitor tours, and assist in educational workshops.

Businesses and individuals can sponsor exhibits or educational programs. Sponsorship opportunities provide high visibility and show your commitment to education and community engagement.

We appreciate all the help we have received from volunteers and sponsors over the past few years.

Thanks to your support, we now offer monthly free admission days, welcoming over 5,000 additional visitors, and have doubled our Science After School program's reach, providing vital STEM education to underserved communities.
