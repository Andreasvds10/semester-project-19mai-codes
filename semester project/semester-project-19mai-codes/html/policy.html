Privacy Policy

The community Science Museum is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our website.

Information we collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you register for events, subscribe to our newsletters, or participate in our interactive features. This information may include your name, email address, and phone number.

Your choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but it may limit your ability to use some of our website's features.

Change to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
