Our Privacy Policy Promise

At the Community Science Museum, your privacy is super important to us! Here's how we take care of the information you share with us.

What Information we collect

When you sign up for fun stuff like events or newsletters, we collect the details you give us, like your name, email, and phone number.

Your choices

You can decide not to share some information with us, but that might mean you can’t use all the fun features on our website.

Updates to Our Policy

Now and then, we might change our privacy rules. We’ll always let you know on this page when we make changes, so it’s a good idea to check here once in a while.
